Company

CAREPATH is a leading online medical channeling platform in Sri Lanka, dedicated to making healthcare accessible and convenient. Our goal is to connect patients with the right healthcare professionals, pharmacies, and home care services through a seamless digital experience.

Our Mission

We strive to enhance healthcare accessibility by providing a user-friendly platform that allows patients to book doctor appointments, consult via chat and video calls, and access trusted pharmacies and home care services.

Our Vision

To revolutionize healthcare in Sri Lanka by leveraging technology to provide efficient, reliable, and affordable medical services for all.

What We Offer

  • Doctor Channeling - Easily book appointments with top doctors across Sri Lanka.
  • Virtual Consultations - Chat and video call with doctors from the comfort of your home.
  • Pharmacy Directory - Find trusted pharmacies for your medical needs.
  • Home Care Services - Access professional home nursing and caregiving services.

Why Choose Us?

  • Convenience - Book appointments and consult doctors anytime, anywhere.
  • Trusted Network - Connect with verified healthcare providers.
  • Secure & Reliable - Your health information is protected with strict security measures.
  • User-Friendly Experience - A seamless and easy-to-use platform for all age groups.
